The grid on the left shows my time usage, while the grid on the right shows time usage of ordinary full-time college students, using data from American Time Use Survey 2014

The line chart on the bottom left shows how much time I spent on each category for each day of the week.

The bar chart on the bottom right compares the total time I spent on each category of activities (bars with solid color) and the counterpart of students in the Survey (bars with textures).

The visualization is written in D3.js. Source code is available on Github.
